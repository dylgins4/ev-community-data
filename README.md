# Smart EV Emergency Response Guide — Community Vehicle Data

Help us add more electric vehicles to the [Smart EV Emergency Response Guide](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.example.ev_mobile) app — used by first responders to quickly look up EV rescue procedures during emergencies.

**We need rescue sheet PDFs for EVs not yet in the app. Want to add your vehicle???** 

---

## How to Add a Vehicle

1. **Find a rescue sheet PDF** for an EV not in our list below (see [Where to Find Them](#where-to-find-rescue-sheets))
2. **Upload it** to the [`submissions/`](submissions/) folder by [clicking here to upload](../../upload/main/submissions) — or open an [Issue](../../issues/new) and attach the PDF there
3. **Tell us** the make, model, and where you found it
4. **We handle the rest** — we'll review it, process it, and push it to the app

Once approved, the vehicle will appear in the app for all users automatically.

---

## Where to Find Rescue Sheets

Rescue sheets are official manufacturer documents that show first responders how to safely handle an EV. Here are some places to find them:

- [Euro NCAP Rescue Sheets](https://www.euroncap.com/en/vehicle-safety/rescue-sheets/) — large free database
- [NFPA Emergency Field Guide](https://www.nfpa.org/training-and-events/by-topic/alternative-fuel-vehicle-safety-training/emergency-field-guide) — US-focused
- Many manufacturers publish them on their websites — try googling **"[car name] rescue sheet PDF"**

We're looking for PDFs that include things like:
- Where the high-voltage battery and cables are
- Safe cut zones for extrication
- How to disable the high-voltage system
- Fire and submersion response steps

## Submission Tips

- **English PDFs only** for now
- **One vehicle per submission**
- **Official documents only** — from the manufacturer or a recognized safety organization
- **Please don't submit documents behind paywalls**
